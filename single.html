About Russ

I live and work as a photographer, whitewater raft guide, and wilderness thrill-seeker in Bend, Oregon. I grew up in the Desert Southwest town of Santa Fe, New Mexico in a family of outdoors lovers and adventurers. I spent my youth exploring the deserts, mountains, coasts, and forest of the American West before eventually relocating to the Columbia River Gorge and Central Cascades.



My love for the outdoors and the beauty of the West has inspired my hobbies, passions and the images I create. My photography is an extension of the love I have for nature and wilderness-pursuits: backcountry skiing in the Cascades and Rockies, canyoneering through the slot canyons of the Colorado Plateau, mountain biking and hiking on the trails of the American West, kite surfing and kayaking in the Columbia River Gorge.
